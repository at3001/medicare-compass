Medicare 是什么？What is Medicare?

Medicare 是针对 65 岁或以上人群的美国联邦健康保险计划。 超过一半的美国人通过雇主获得健康保险； 然而，许多雇主在员工退休后停止提供这些福利。 这就是为什么许多退休人员一旦符合资格就加入医疗保险，这样他们在停止工作时可以继续获得负担得起的医疗保险。

Medicare is a US federal health insurance program for people age 65 or older. Over half of Americans receive their health insurance through their employer; however, many employers stop offering these benefits after their employees retire. This is why many retirees enroll in Medicare once they become eligible – so they can continue receiving affordable healthcare insurance when they stop working.

Medicare由哪几部分组成？

Medicare 有 4 个主要部分，每个部分涵盖不同的医疗保健服务。 了解不同的部分非常重要，这样您就可以选择一个涵盖您需要的计划并避免意外费用。

What are the parts of Medicare?

Medicare has 4 main parts -- Part A, B, C, and D -- and each part covers a different set of healthcare services. It’s important to understand the different parts so you can pick a plan that covers what you need and avoid unexpected costs.
